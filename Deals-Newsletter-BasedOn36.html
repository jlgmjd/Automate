Featured Budtender
 
 

Meet Tiffany

Tiffany is a Lead budtender at Cannabis Oasis in Tacoma, WA. Tiffany is a huge music fan and dancer who just happens to hold court at one of Tacoma’s finest dispensaries. I asked her about the cannabis scene in Tacoma and Tiffany had this to say “the Tacoma cannabis community is a colorful group. We interact with people from all walks of life. Everyone with a different story to tell. But the one thing that unites us is cannabis.”

As we do with all our budtenders we wanted to get to know what Tiffany is smoking so we asked:

  Favorite Strain: My favorite strain is LA Confidential.  It helps me unwind at the end of the day.   

  Favorite Stoner Movie: Pineapple Express   

  Final Question: If you are gonna smoke a joint with one person DEAD or ALIVE, who would it be and why? My last joint would be a cotton candy Mr. Twister with my friend Jena.  No one can make me laugh like she does.  

Please stop in and introduce yourself to Tiffany next time you are in Tacoma or even better, give her a follow on Instagram at @free.spirit.82
